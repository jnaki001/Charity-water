Our Story

At charity: water, we believe in a world where everyone has access to clean water. Our nonprofit organization brings clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries. With the help of generous supporters, we fund water projects that transform lives, empower communities, and foster sustainable development.

Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina can go to school and her family is healthier and happier.
